Testimonial - Tim Diggins

When we needed image uploads for a messaging area on a client site, we decided to try out Shubox — just for a month initially. Our plan was we would migrate away from Shubox when we had some feature that they couldn’t provide, or when the price payoff didn’t work any more. We just haven’t needed to migrate — judging from client satisfaction and developer team happiness, this has been a good choice.
